KennyS

唯一K神

Known for being a former Counter-Strike: Source player, kennyS is currently playing for G2 Esports since February 2017 after a player swap between EnVyUs and G2 Esports. kennyS was playing for EnVyUs since a player exchange with Titan.[1]. He was on Titan since May 2014[2], coming back after leaving them on May 2013, when he was replaced by Richard "shox" Papillon [3], who later left Titan to join Clan-Mystik, giving kennyS the opportunity to get recruited back into Titan. He is regarded as, by his fans and other players, one of the quickest and most skilled AWPers, if not even the best AWPer in the game.
